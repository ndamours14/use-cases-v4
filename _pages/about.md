## What is the AV Annotation Use Case Recipe Book?
Annotating audiovisual (AV) materials is a useful, multidisciplinary academic practice. AV annotation can be performed using a variety of tools, including **AVAnnotate**. 

AVAnnotate is a powerful tool that can be used for many purposes, across many fields of study. Much like a culinary recipe book, the Use Case Recipe Book aims to provide users with an informative and inspiring list of potential uses for AVAnnotate, with helpful lists of “ingredients” (required elements), simple to-do steps, and example projects. This recipe book is not an exhaustive list of all of AVAnnotate’s uses; instead, it aims to inspire. The recipes on this website can be followed precisely, or used as a jumping-off point for your own AV projects.
